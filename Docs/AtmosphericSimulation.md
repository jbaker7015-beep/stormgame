# Atmospheric Simulation

# Philosophy

StormGame uses semi-realistic atmospheric simulation.

The goal is:
- believable weather behavior
- fun gameplay
- dynamic ecosystems

Gameplay readability is more important than scientific perfection.

---

# Core Atmospheric Systems

## Humidity

Generated by:
- oceans
- lakes
- rivers
- forests

Humidity fuels:
- cloud growth
- rainfall
- storm sustainability

---

## Heat

Generated by:
- sunlight
- urban areas
- deserts
- warm oceans

Heat creates:
- atmospheric lift
- instability
- storm intensification

---

## Instability

Represents atmospheric imbalance.

Generated through:
- temperature differences
- rising warm air
- conflicting air masses

Instability enables:
- thunderstorm formation
- severe weather
- rapid growth

---

## Wind

Wind influences:
- storm movement
- rotation
- spread
- environmental interaction

---

## Wind Shear

Controls:
- supercell formation
- tornado potential
- storm rotation

Higher wind shear increases severe weather potential.

---

# Environmental Interactions

## Mountains
Create:
- lift

- storm redirection

---

## Oceans
Provide:
- humidity
- heat retention
- hurricane growth

---

## Cities
Generate:
- urban heat
- population risk
- infrastructure targets

---

## Forests
Provide:
- moisture
- wildfire interactions

# Atmospheric Lifecycle

1. heat accumulation
2. humidity gathering
3. instability formation
4. cloud development
5. storm intensification
6. severe weather evolution
7. dissipation

# Simulation Goals

The atmosphere should feel:
- alive
- dynamic
- reactive
- emergent

Every match should evolve differently.
