# Monetization Design

# Philosophy

Monetization should never feel pay-to-win.

Gameplay balance and fairness are top priorities.

Purchases should focus primarily on:
- cosmetics
- visual customization
- optional convenience

Player skill and strategy should always matter more than spending.

---

# Possible Monetization Models

## Premium Game
One-time purchase with future expansions.

---

## Free-to-Play
Optional cosmetic purchases and progression systems.

---

# Cosmetic Systems

Potential cosmetics:
- storm skins
- cloud coloration
- lightning colors
- radar themes
- UI themes
- weather agency uniforms

Cosmetics should not affect gameplay power.

---

# Progression Philosophy

Gameplay progression should always remain achievable without spending money.

Avoid:
- paywalls
- pay-to-win mechanics
- excessive grind design

---

# Battle Pass Possibility

Potential future seasonal system:
- cosmetic rewards
- weather-themed seasons
- storm events
- environmental themes

---

# Expansion Possibilities

Future expansions could include:
- new storm types
- new regions
- new weather systems
- advanced atmospheric mechanics

---

# Community Philosophy

The player community should feel:
- respected
- fairly treated
- long-term supported

Monetization should support development without damaging gameplay integrity.

---

# Long-Term Monetization Goals

Create sustainable development funding while preserving:
- gameplay fairness
- player trust
- long-term replayability
- ecosystem balance
