# Prototype Goals

# Philosophy

StormGame should be developed through small playable prototypes.

**Current era:** Storm simulation (S-phases) — see [StormFeaturesImplementationPlan.md](StormFeaturesImplementationPlan.md).

Each prototype should:
- prove a gameplay concept
- remain simple
- be testable quickly
- avoid unnecessary complexity

The focus is **credible storm routing and modes**, not arcade WASD feel.

**Legacy prototypes 1–5** below are largely complete; **prototype 6+** on this page are superseded by S-phases until storm slice ships.

---

# Prototype 1 - Atmospheric Movement

Goal:
Create a controllable moisture pocket.

Features:
- movement
- camera controls
- basic UI
- humidity collection
- heat collection

Success Condition:
Player movement feels responsive and satisfying.

---

# Prototype 2 - Atmospheric Growth

Goal:
Introduce storm growth systems.

Features:
- energy accumulation
- instability generation
- size scaling
- visual cloud growth

Success Condition:
Storm visibly evolves based on player actions.

---

# Prototype 3 - Early Weather Effects

Goal:
Add simple weather systems.

Features:
- rainfall
- wind
- lightning
- environmental effects

Success Condition:
Storm feels alive and atmospheric.

---

# Prototype 4 - Evolution System

Goal:
Implement evolution stages.

Features:
- stage transitions
- visual transformations

Success Condition:
Player feels meaningful progression.

---

# Prototype 5 - AI Storm Ecosystem

Goal:
Introduce AI-controlled storms.

Features:
- resource gathering
- AI evolution
- ecosystem competition

Success Condition:
World feels alive even in single-player.

---

# Prototype 6 - Destruction Systems

Goal:
Allow storms to impact the environment.

Features:
- destructible objects
- debris
- infrastructure damage

Success Condition:
Storm power feels meaningful.

---

# Prototype 7 - Weather Agency Gameplay

Goal:
Add asymmetric gameplay.

Features:
- forecasting
- warnings
- evacuations

Success Condition:

---

# Development Rules

- avoid overengineering
- keep prototypes small
- prioritize gameplay feel
- test often
- commit frequently
