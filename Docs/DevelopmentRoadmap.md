# Development Roadmap

# Development Philosophy

StormGame should be built iteratively.

The goal is to:
- create small playable milestones
- avoid feature overload
- test systems early
- prioritize gameplay feel

Multiplayer should NOT be the initial focus.

---

# Phase 1 - Foundation

Goal:
Create a controllable atmospheric entity.

Features:
- basic movement
- camera controls
- moisture collection
- heat collection
- basic HUD
- energy meter

Success Condition:
Player can move and absorb atmospheric resources.

---

# Phase 2 - Atmospheric Simulation

Goal:
Introduce basic weather systems.

Features:
- humidity zones
- heat zones
- instability generation
- environmental interactions

Success Condition:
Storm growth feels dynamic and environmental.

---

# Phase 3 - Evolution System

Goal:
Storm evolves through stages.

Features:
- moisture pocket
- updraft
- cumulus cloud
- thunderstorm

Success Condition:
Player experiences visual and gameplay progression.

---

# Phase 4 - Visual Effects

Goal:
Storms visually feel alive.

Features:
- cloud particles
- rainfall
- wind effects
- lighting
- atmospheric audio

Success Condition:
Storms feel immersive and readable.

---

# Phase 5 - AI Systems

Goal:
Populate world with intelligent storms.

Features:
- AI storms
- ecosystem competition
- resource seeking
- basic difficulty levels

Success Condition:
Matches feel alive without multiplayer.

---

# Phase 6 - Destruction Systems

Goal:
Storms impact the environment.

Features:
- building damage
- debris
- population effects
- infrastructure systems

Success Condition:
Storm growth feels meaningful and dangerous.

---

# Phase 7 - Weather Agency Systems

Goal:
Introduce asymmetric gameplay.

Features:
- forecasting
- warning systems
- evacuations
- infrastructure defense

Success Condition:
Storms and agencies create strategic gameplay tension.

---

# Phase 8 - Storm Specialization

Goal:
Branching evolution paths.

Features:
- tornado path
- hurricane path
- blizzard path
- lightning storm path

Success Condition:
Different storm types feel unique.

---

# Phase 9 - Multiplayer

Goal:
Online ecosystem gameplay.

Features:
- multiplayer matches
- synchronized weather systems
- PvP storm competition
- co-op agency gameplay

Success Condition:
Stable multiplayer ecosystem.

---

# Phase 10 - Polish

Goal:
Refinement and replayability.

Features:
- progression
- cosmetics
- advanced AI
- balance
- optimization
- additional maps

Success Condition:
Complete gameplay experience.
