# AI Systems Design

## Overview

StormGame supports both multiplayer and single-player gameplay through advanced AI-controlled systems.

AI players are intended to:
- populate matches
- create living weather ecosystems
- provide offline gameplay
- improve replayability
- scale difficulty naturally

Both Storm Players and Weather Agency roles can be controlled by AI.

---

# Design Philosophy

AI should feel:
- dynamic
- believable
- adaptive
- ecosystem-driven

AI should not rely on unfair cheating mechanics.

Higher difficulty AI should demonstrate:
- better prediction
- faster reactions
- improved resource management
- more intelligent decision making

---

# AI Storm Systems

Storm AI controls evolving weather systems.

AI storms should:
- seek atmospheric resources
- evolve strategically
- react to environmental conditions
- compete against other storms

Storm AI behavior changes based on storm type.

---

# Storm AI Behaviors

## Resource Seeking
AI prioritizes:
- humidity
- heat
- instability zones
- ocean regions
- urban heat areas

---

## Survival Behavior
AI attempts to:
- avoid dissipation
- preserve storm energy
- maintain favorable atmospheric conditions

---

## Aggression Behavior
Advanced storms may:
- compete for territory
- absorb weaker storms
- target high-value population zones

---

# Weather Agency AI

Weather Agency AI simulates disaster response organizations.

Agency AI:
- tracks storms
- issues warnings
- evacuates civilians
- reinforces infrastructure

Agency AI should make the world feel active even without human agency players.

---

# Forecasting AI

Agency AI uses:
- radar analysis
- storm trajectory prediction
- atmospheric condition monitoring

Prediction accuracy scales with difficulty level.

---

# Difficulty Levels

## Easy
- slow reactions
- basic forecasting
- inefficient resource usage

---

## Normal
- balanced decision making
- moderate forecasting accuracy
- reasonable strategic choices

---

## Hard
- advanced forecasting
- optimized evolution paths
- adaptive behavior
- intelligent mitigation planning

---

## Extreme
- highly efficient optimization
- advanced atmospheric prediction
- aggressive strategic behavior
- near-professional forecasting quality

Extreme AI should still follow the same game rules as players.

---

# Ecosystem Simulation

AI storms interact with:
- terrain
- atmospheric systems
- cities
- civilians
- other storms

The world should feel alive even without human interaction.

---

# Long-Term AI Goals

Future AI systems may include:
- machine-learning-inspired storm behavior
- adaptive forecasting systems
- personality-based storm patterns
- historical weather-inspired AI models

The ultimate goal is to create a dynamic living weather ecosystem where every match evolves differently.
